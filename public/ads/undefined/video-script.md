## Full Script
Banks aren't rejecting your business. They're rejecting your file. [PAUSE] And nobody told you that.

[DIRECT TO CAMERA] What I do is pull the file, read it the way an underwriter reads it, and tell you exactly what's triggering the decline. Then we restructure your capital position so the next application lands differently.

[LEAN IN] The operators I work with aren't getting lucky. They're getting positioned correctly — and then deploying $150K to $500K in growth-ready capital inside 30 to 45 days.

[DIRECT TO CAMERA, slow] If you want to know what's in your file that's blocking approval — DM me the word FUNDED. I'll look at it and tell you straight.

## B-Roll Callouts
1. HOOK (0–5s): Stark black frame. White serif text appears word by word: 'Banks aren't rejecting your business. They're rejecting your file.' No logo. No music. Talking head cuts in at second 3 — Abu on camera, plain background, no studio polish.
2. PROBLEM (5–20s): Talking head — Abu direct to lens, sound-off captions carrying every word. Cut to a B-roll still: a declined application letter, partially visible, no personal info. Back to face. Tight frame, no movement.
3. BRIDGE (20–35s): Stay on talking head. Optional: quick text overlay listing '5 data points' in plain white on black — not animated, just cut. Reinforces the mechanism without leaving the face for long.
4. PROOF (35–50s): Return to tight talking-head. If a real client outcome exists, cut to a phone screenshot of a funded amount (blurred name) for 2 seconds, then back to face. If not, stay on Abu — his calm certainty IS the proof.
5. CTA (50–60s): Extreme close-up or slight lean toward camera. Text overlay at bottom: 'DM me FUNDED' in gold or white bold. Hold on face for full 8 seconds. No music swell, no animation. Let the silence work.
