# Storyboard — Denied Operator — undefined

Total duration: 70s

## Shot 1 — ECU talking head — extreme close-up, slight dutch angle, harsh side lighting (5s)

**On-screen text:** Banks aren't rejecting your business.
**Action:** Abu stares directly into lens, dead still. No movement. White text slams in at the cut. Pause held for effect — uncomfortable silence before the next line lands.

## Shot 2 — Text overlay on black — full screen, no speaker visible (4s)

**On-screen text:** They're rejecting your FILE.
**Action:** Black screen. Gold text appears word by word — not animated, just cuts. Hard pause. Then: 'And nobody told you that.' in smaller white text below. No music. Dead air.

## Shot 3 — Direct to camera — medium close-up, leaning slightly forward, flat dark background (8s)

**On-screen text:** $600K/year. Bank still said no?
**Action:** Abu speaks directly: 'If your business clears $600K a year and a bank still said no —' then leans in slightly, 'that's not a cash flow problem. That's a positioning problem.' On-screen text appears as he says the revenue figure, then cuts away.

## Shot 4 — B-roll — close shot of a physical loan document stamped DECLINED in red, shot on a dark desk surface (10s)

**On-screen text:** 5 data points. None of them are on your P&L.
**Action:** Camera slowly pushes in on the denial letter. Abu voiceover: 'Banks don't look at your revenue the way you think they do. They run it through a scoring model — and that model is looking at five specific data points. None of which are on your P&L.' Text overlay holds across the shot.

## Shot 5 — Split screen — left: clean-looking spreadsheet / right: same data with red underwriting flags overlaid (7s)

**On-screen text:** Perfect to you. Broken to them.
**Action:** Abu voiceover continues: 'Your file can look perfect to you and broken to them at the same time.' Left side labeled YOU in white, right side labeled UNDERWRITER in gold. Red X marks appear on the right side at the line: 'broken to them.'

## Shot 6 — B-roll — handheld, slightly gritty. A real operator at a desk reviewing documents, phone nearby, not posed (9s)

**On-screen text:** Real revenue. Real years in. Still declined.
**Action:** Abu voiceover: 'That's why solid operators — businesses with real revenue, real customers, real years in — get declined. Not because they're bad bets. Because their file wasn't structured for the way banks score capital readiness.' Operator looks up from documents — defeated expression. No performance, no stock-photo gloss.

## Shot 7 — Direct to camera — medium shot, deliberate pacing, no text overlay (9s)

**On-screen text:** (none)
**Action:** Abu speaks plainly and slowly: 'What I do is pull the file, read it the way an underwriter reads it, and tell you exactly what's triggering the decline. Then we restructure your capital position so the next application lands differently.' No cuts. Single continuous take. Stillness is the emphasis.

## Shot 8 — ECU talking head — lean in, close on face, gold accent border frame appears at edge of frame (7s)

**On-screen text:** $150K–$500K. 30–45 days.
**Action:** Abu leans in: 'The operators I work with aren't getting lucky. They're getting positioned correctly — and then deploying $150K to $500K in growth-ready capital inside 30 to 45 days.' Numbers appear on screen as he speaks them. Gold frame pulses in once, then holds.

## Shot 9 — Direct to camera — slow, deliberate, pulled back slightly to medium, minimal lighting (11s)

**On-screen text:** DM me the word FUNDED.
**Action:** Abu speaks slow and flat: 'If you want to know what's in your file that's blocking approval — DM me the word FUNDED. I'll look at it and tell you straight.' Text appears as a single line, white on black, at the bottom of the frame. No flourish. Word FUNDED appears in gold. Holds for two beats after he finishes speaking.
